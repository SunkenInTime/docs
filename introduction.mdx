Icarus is an interactive strategy creation tool for Valorant players. It focuses on a robust map drawing system that lets teams create, save, and iterate on strategies locally.

## Features

Icarus provides everything you need to plan and visualize Valorant strategies:

- **Interactive map drawing and annotations** - Draw paths, mark positions, and annotate maps with a full suite of drawing tools
- **Save, load, and organize strategies locally** - Keep all your strategies organized in folders with Hive local storage
- **Agent and ability helpers** - Quickly add agent icons and ability markers to your strategies
- **Desktop-focused UX for planning sessions** - Built specifically for team planning sessions with a clean, efficient interface

## Tech stack

Icarus is built with modern cross-platform technologies:

- **Flutter (Dart)** - Cross-platform desktop framework for smooth, native performance
- **Riverpod** - State management for reactive UI updates
- **Hive** - Fast, local-first storage for strategies and folders

## Why Icarus?

Whether you're a competitive team planning your next tournament match or a casual group coordinating ranked games, Icarus gives you the tools to:

- Visualize complex strategies with precision
- Iterate quickly on tactics and positioning
- Save and organize unlimited strategies locally
- Share strategies with your team via exported `.ica` files
